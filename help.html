System Requirements

  1. Operating Systems: Windows 2000 or higher; Mac OS X (10.1 or higher); Linux
  2. Display: Minimum 1024x768 required. Higher resolution is recommended.
  3. Browsers:
    1. Windows: Firefox 2 or later, Internet Explorer 7 or later
    2. MacOS: Firefox 2 or later, Safari, Mozilla 1.7 or later
    3. Linux: Firefox 2 or later, Mozilla 1.7 or later
  4. JavaScript: Your browser must have JavaScript enabled

If you have further questions about Southside Chicago, please send an e-mail to Josh Stanley, mjs2131@columbia.edu.
